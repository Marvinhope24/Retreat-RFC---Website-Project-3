What's new to the club?

August 15, 2022

Earlier this week, our first and second team signed agreements regarding sponsorships.
The teams received full kits, which contains a shirt, shorts, and socks.
Many players wear other protective equipment, such as a mouthguard, light padding, and headgear. Cones and tackling pads are important tools for training and practicing rugby, which is another sponsorship we are working on.
